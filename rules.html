Game Rules

Welcome to America Says! Here are the rules:

  • Each team takes turns guessing answers based on the category prompt.
  • You earn points for each correct answer:
    • Category 1 & 2 - 100 points for each correct answer. 1000 bonus points if all 7 answers are correct.
    • Category 3 & 4 - 200 points for each correct answer. 2000 bonus points if all 7 answers are correct
    • Category 5 & 6 - 300 points for each correct answer. 3000 bonus points if all 7 answers are correct
  • The 30 second timer starts when the blanks are displayed.
  • If the original team fails to answer all, the other team can steal.
  • The stealing team only earns regular pointsâ€”no bonus points.
  • There is a bonus round! The team with the most points after the 6 rounds gets access to the bonus round!
